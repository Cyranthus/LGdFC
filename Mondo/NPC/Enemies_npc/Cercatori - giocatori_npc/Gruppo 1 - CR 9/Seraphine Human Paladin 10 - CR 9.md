TACTICS

**Before Combat** The paladin casts [bull’s strength](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/bull-s-strength) and [delay poison](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/delay-poison).

**During Combat** The paladin disarms foes to capture and question them. The exceptions are mindless creatures, evil [outsiders](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/creature-types#TOC-Outsider), and [undead](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/creature-types#TOC-Undead), all of which he destroys on the spot.
