TACTICS

**Before Combat** The druid casts [barkskin](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/barkskin) and [cat’s grace](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/cat-s-grace). She casts [endure elements](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/e/endure-elements) every morning.

**During Combat** The druid keeps her [distance](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/magic-weapons/magic-weapon-special-abilities/distance) from opponents. She casts [freedom of movement](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/freedom-of-movement), then wild shapes into an [air elemental](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/outsiders/elemental/elemental-air) to fly away. Once she feels she has the tactical advantage, she relies on summon spells and ranged abilities to avoid melee.
