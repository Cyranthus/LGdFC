TACTICS

**Before Combat** The wizard casts [mage armor](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/mage-armor) from her [wand](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/wands).

**During Combat** The wizard prefers to prank others rather than cause deliberate harm. She casts [greater invisibility](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/invisibility), then harasses and annoys her targets with [glitterdust](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/g/glitterdust), [grease](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/g/grease), [major image](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/major-image), [persistent image](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/p/persistent-image), and [solid fog](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/solid-fog). If attacked with lethal force, she retaliates with [cloudkill](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/cloudkill), [empowered](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/metamagic-feats/empower-spell-metamagic) [scorching ray](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/scorching-ray), and [magic missile](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/magic-missile).

Base Statistics

Without [mage armor](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/mage-armor), the wizard’s statistics are **AC** 16, [touch](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Touch-Attacks) 15, [flat-footed](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Flat-Footed) 13.
