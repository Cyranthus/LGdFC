TACTICS

**During Combat** The barbarian charges into the heart of battle, using one end of his double axe two-handed when charging or moving, and switching back to a double weapon grip when making full attacks. He uses his leaping and [Acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics) to enter melee quickly, using his [no escape](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/barbarian/rage-powers/paizo-rage-powers/no-escape-ex) ability to chase down cowards.
