#### **Mistero dell'Oracolo**

*   **Mistero**: **Battaglia** (orientato al combattimento)
    
    *   **Rivelazioni**:
        
        1.  _Battlecry (Ex)_: As a [standard action](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Standard-Actions), you can unleash an inspiring battlecry. All allies within 100 feet who hear your cry gain a +1 [morale bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Morale-Bonus) on attack rolls, skill checks, and [saving throws](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Saving-Throws) for a number of rounds equal to your [Charisma](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Charisma-Cha-) modifier. At 10th level, this bonus increases to +2. You can use this ability once per day, plus one additional time per day at 5th level and for every five levels thereafter.
            
        2.  _Combat Healer (Su)_: Whenever you cast a cure spell (a spell with “cure” in its name), you can cast it as a [swift action](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Swift-Actions), as if using the [Quicken Spell](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/metamagic-feats/quicken-spell-metamagic) feat, by expending two spell slots. This does not increase the level of the spell. You can use this ability once per day at 7th level and one additional time per day for every four levels beyond 7th. You must be at least 7th level to select this revelation.
            

#### **Maledizione dell'Oracolo**

#### **Accursed**

You are cursed with misfortune and sorrow, and you cannot gain benefit from morale bonuses.  
However, you gain a +4 bonus to all saving throws against curse effects.  
At 5th level, add [_ill omen_](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/ill-omen) to your list of spells known  
At 10th level, add greater [_brand_](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/brand) to your list of spells known  
At 15th level, you are immune to curse effects except for your own oracle curse.
