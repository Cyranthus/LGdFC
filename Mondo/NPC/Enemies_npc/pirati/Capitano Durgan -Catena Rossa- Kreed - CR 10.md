TACTICS

**Before Combat** The rogue applies blue whinnis [poison](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/afflictions/poison) to his [rapier](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/equipment/weapons/weapon-descriptions/rapier) and [dagger](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/equipment/weapons/weapon-descriptions/dagger).

**During Combat** The rogue focuses on one target, using [Power Attack](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/power-attack-combat), [sneak attack](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/rogue#TOC-Sneak-Attack), and the ongoing effects of his [bleeding attack](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/rogue/rogue-talents/paizo-rogue-talents/bleeding-attack-ex) ability and blue whinnis [poison](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/afflictions/poison) to deal as much damage as possible in a short amount of time. If the target tries to flee, the contract killer uses [Stand Still](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/stand-still-combat) to prevent escape, or throws his poisoned [dagger](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/equipment/weapons/weapon-descriptions/dagger).
