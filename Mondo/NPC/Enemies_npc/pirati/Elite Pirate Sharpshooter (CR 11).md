TACTICS

**Before Combat** During alerts, elite pirate sharpshooters apply oil of [flame arrow](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/flame-arrow) to their own bolts and may share the effect with the arrows or bolts of nearby allies under their command. Treat the projectiles of a pirate crew or similar [as 30 arrows for the purpose of such sharing.](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/npc-s/npcs-cr-11/elite-pirate-sharpshooter-cr-11/%22/bestiary/monster-listings/humanoids/troop%3C/a)

**During Combat** An elite pirate sharpshooter uses her bleeding wound deed on the first target she hits each round, dealing 6 points of [bleed](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Bleed) damage (0 grit cost). If the target is already taking hit point [bleed](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Bleed) damage, she spends 1 grit point to give the target 1 point of [Constitution](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/ability-scores#TOC-Constitution-Con-) [bleed](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Bleed). She typically uses her sharp shoot deed to resolve her final iterative attack as a ranged [touch attack](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Touch-Attacks). She uses shooter’s resolve as needed against foes using [blur](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/blur) or [concealment](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Concealment) or [cover](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Cover), while moving to get a better vantage point for future attacks. If she locates [invisible](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Invisible) targets, she uses her tangle bolts to [pin](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Pinned) them down.
