TACTICS

**Before Combat** These skulking [goblins](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/humanoids/goblin) always use [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) when moving around [Fort](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Fortitude) Scurvy, even when the castle is not on alert. If encountered during an alert, a pirate grenadier will have used extracts of [false life](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/false-life), [heroism](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/heroism), [protection from energy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/p/protection-from-energy) (usually fire, unless the PCs have been observed using a different energy type), and [see invisibility](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/see-invisibility). If PCs are known to have entered the castle, they also use [cat’s](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/animals/cat) [grace](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/g/grace), [death ward](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/death-ward), [expeditious retreat](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/e/expeditious-retreat), [invisibility](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/invisibility), [reduce person](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/r/reduce-person), and shield.

**During Combat** A pirate grenadier uses [haste](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/haste) and [thorn body](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/t/thorn-body) before entering combat and uses [Rapid Shot](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/rapid-shot-combat) to hurl 4 bombs per round, starting with force bombs and tanglefoot bombs to immobilize enemies. They use [dragon](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/rules-for-monsters/creature-types#TOC-Dragon) breath or [vomit swarm](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/v/vomit-swarm) against groups of enemies, especially those unable to move, and use [fly](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/fly) if needed to get around obstacles.

**Morale** If brought below 20 [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points), a pirate grenadier flees using [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) or [invisibility](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/invisibility).
