TACTICS

**Before Combat** During alerts, pirate sharpshooters drink their [elixir of hiding](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/wondrous-items/wondrous-items/e-g/elixir-of-hiding), increasing their [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) bonus to +37.

**During Combat** Pirate sharpshooters use [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) in combat, preferring to make a single attack each round with [Deadly Aim](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/deadly-aim-combat) and [Vital Strike](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/vital-strike-combat), and then [returning](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/r/returning-weapon) to hiding (taking only a -10 penalty on [Stealth](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/stealth) checks when sniping). After 5 shots, they keep hidden while taking a [full-round action](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Full-Round-Actions) to reload their repeating crossbow. They use their sharp shoot deed against heavily armored targets. If intruders are largely [human](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/core-races/human), they load their case of [human](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/core-races/human) [bane](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/bane) bolts, or begin combat with those bolts loaded if an alert has been raised, focusing their shots on [human](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/core-races/human) PCs. Pirate sharpshooters use the full attack action against opponents that keep their [distance](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/magic-weapons/magic-weapon-special-abilities/distance), remaining beyond [sneak attack](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/rogue#TOC-Sneak-Attack) range.
