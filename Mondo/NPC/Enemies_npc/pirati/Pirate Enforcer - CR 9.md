TACTICS

**During Combat** Pirate enforcers keep to the shadows while letting their allies draw enemies towards them. They then use their whips to [silence](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/silence) spellcasters with [trip](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Trip) and [grapple](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Grapple) [combat maneuvers](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Combat-Maneuvers), tying them up and moving on to new opponents. They normally deal [nonlethal damage](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Nonlethal-Damage) with their whips in order to use their [Enforcer](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/combat-feats/enforcer-combat) feat to strike fear into their targets, but switch to lethal damage and [sneak attacks](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/rogue#TOC-Sneak-Attack) when enemies stand firm. They use their [dust of appearance](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/wondrous-items/wondrous-items/c-d/dust-of-appearance) to eliminate defensive illusions like [invisibility](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/invisibility), [displacement](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/d/displacement), and [mirror image](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/mirror-image) before attacking spellcasters.
