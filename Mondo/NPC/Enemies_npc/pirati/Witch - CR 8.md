TACTICS

**Before Combat** This legendary witch casts [greater false life](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/false-life) and [mage armor](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/mage-armor). These spells are represented in the above stat block.

**During Combat** This legendary witch spend their actions to support others nearby if available by casting spells such as [haste](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/h/haste), and then attempt to isolate and remove high threat targets with [slay living](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/slay-living), masochistic shadow or their hand of death ability. If surrounded or greatly outnumbered, this legendary witch will attempt to fly above enemies and use [web](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/w/web) and [cloudkill](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/cloudkill) to eliminate large groups.

**Morale** This legendary witch will rarely fight to the death but is unwilling to leave friends behind. They will use their few area control spells such as [web](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/w/web) to aid in the escape of close allies. This legendary witch begins planning or maneuvering for escape when below 20 [hit points](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Hit-Points).

SPECIAL ABILITIES

#### Resist Level Drain (Ex)

A dhampir takes no penalties from [energy drain](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/e/energy-drain) effects, though they can still be killed if they accrue more [negative levels](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/special-abilities#TOC-Energy-Drain-and-Negative-Levels) then they have [Hit Dice](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Hit-Dice-HD-). After 24 hours, any [negative levels](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/special-abilities#TOC-Energy-Drain-and-Negative-Levels) a [dhampir](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/races/other-races/featured-races/arg-dhampir) takes are removed without the need for an additional saving throw.

#### Undead Resistance (Ex)

Dhampirs gain a +2 [racial bonus](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/basics-ability-scores/glossary#TOC-Racial-Bonus) on saving throws against [disease](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/afflictions/diseases) and [mind-affecting](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic#TOC-Magic-Descriptor-Mind-Affecting) effects.

#### Boon

If this legendary witch is friendly to the PCs, which can be accomplished by providing aid in combat or procuring magical supplies for one of their needs, and succeeding at a DC 15 [Diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy) check, they will provide [potions](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/potions) to the PCs at a 10% discount. They produce the [potions](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/potions) themselves and can provide [potions](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/potions) for any of the spells on the witch spell list, though may ask for the PCs to procure a [scroll of](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/scrolls) the spell if it is obscure or rare at the GM’s discretion, subtracting the cost of the [scroll](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/scrolls) from the first [potion](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic-items/potions) produced.

This legendary witch can also use [speak with dead](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/s/speak-with-dead) on the PCs behalf and offers their full services as a spellcaster so long as the PCs remain in the same area or cross paths.
