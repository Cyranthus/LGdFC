TACTICS

**Before Combat** The druid casts [barkskin](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/barkskin). He also casts [ironwood](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/i/ironwood) on his breastplate twice per month, and [endure elements](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/e/endure-elements) every morning. **During Combat** The [druid](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/classes/core-classes/druid) wild shapes into a Huge [fire elemental](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/outsiders/elemental/elemental-fire) when casting offensive spells, and wild shapes into a Huge [earth elemental](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/bestiary/monster-listings/outsiders/elemental/elemental-earth) when entering melee. He opens with spells such as [fire storm](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/fire-storm) and [empowered](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/feats/metamagic-feats/empower-spell-metamagic) [fireball](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/fireball). Before entering melee, he casts [cat’s grace](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/c/cat-s-grace), [freedom of movement](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/f/freedom-of-movement), and [greater magic fang](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/m/magic-fang) on himself.

Base Statistics

Without [barkskin](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/magic/all-spells/b/barkskin), the druid’s statistics are **AC** 23, [touch](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Touch-Attacks) 13, [flat-footed](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/conditions#TOC-Flat-Footed) 22.
