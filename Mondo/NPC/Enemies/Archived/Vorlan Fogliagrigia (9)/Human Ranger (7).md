SPECIAL ABILITIES

#### **Arc Grenade (Ex)**

An arc grenade is a [splash weapon](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Throw-Splash-Weapon) with a range increment of 20 feet, generally thrown at a grid intersection rather than a creature. When armed and thrown, it detonates at the beginning of the machine slayer’s next turn. The explosion deals 5d6 points of electricity damage to all targets within a 20-foot-radius spread ([Reflex](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/gamemastering/combat#TOC-Reflex) DC 15 half).

An arc grenade weighs 1 pound and is worth 750 gp.
