### **Stregone Umano di Livello 8**

#### **Caratteristiche**

*   **Forza**: 10
    
*   **Destrezza**: 14
    
*   **Costituzione**: 12
    
*   **Intelligenza**: 10
    
*   **Saggezza**: 12
    
*   **Carisma**: 22 (18 base + 2 dall'aumento del livello + oggetto)
    

#### **Character Overview:**

*   **Race:** Human
    
*   **Class:** Sorcerer (Level 9)
    
*   **Bloodline:** Draconic (Red) or Elemental (Fire)
    
    *   These bloodlines enhance your damage-dealing capabilities, especially with fire-based spells.
        

### **Ability Scores (Point Buy Example, 20 points):**

*   **Strength:** 10
    
*   **Dexterity:** 14
    
*   **Constitution:** 14 (you need a decent amount of HP to survive)
    
*   **Intelligence:** 12
    
*   **Wisdom:** 10
    
*   **Charisma:** 18 (Primary stat for Sorcerers, increase it at level 4 and 8)
    

### **Bloodline Powers (Draconic - Red/Fire or Elemental - Fire):**

*   **1st Level (Draconic)**: _Claws_ – For emergency melee situations, gives you natural attacks.
    
*   **3rd Level (Draconic)**: _Breath Weapon_ – A powerful area of effect (AoE) attack that fits with the offensive theme.
    

(For **Elemental - Fire**, focus on bonus damage for fire spells, and the elemental ray as a backup ranged attack.)

### **Feats:**

1.  **Spell Focus (Evocation)** – Boosts the save DC of your evocation spells, which are often damage spells.
    
2.  **Greater Spell Focus (Evocation)** – Further boosts the save DC for your evocation spells.
    
3.  **Metamagic (Empower Spell)** – Allows you to increase the damage of your spells.
    
4.  **Metamagic (Maximize Spell)** – Maximize your spell’s damage output.
    
5.  **Spell Penetration** – Helps you bypass spell resistance.
    
6.  **Greater Spell Penetration** – Further improves your chances to penetrate spell resistance.
    

### **Spells:**

Focus on damage-dealing spells, particularly from the evocation school, and a few versatile control options.

#### **Level 0 (Cantrips):**

*   _Ray of Frost_ – Low-level ranged attack.
    
*   _Acid Splash_ – Another simple ranged touch attack.
    
*   _Detect Magic_ – Essential utility spell.
    
*   _Read Magic_ – Useful for deciphering scrolls.
    

#### **Level 1: - 7**

*   _Magic Missile_ – Guaranteed damage with no attack roll.
    
*   _Burning Hands_ – Great early AoE fire spell.
    
*   _Shocking Grasp_ – Strong single-target spell for touch attacks.
    
*   _Mage Armor_ – Protection if you're caught in a pinch.
    

#### **Level 2: - 5**

*   _Scorching Ray_ – Excellent single-target damage, scaling with level.
    
*   _Mirror Image_ – Defensive spell to avoid damage.
    
*   _Bull’s Strength_ – Buffing allies when not casting offensive spells.
    
*   _Glitterdust_ – Blind enemies and reveal invisible creatures.
    

#### **Level 3: - 3**

*   _Fireball_ – Classic AoE damage spell.
    
*   _Lightning Bolt_ – Another strong AoE spell but in a line rather than a radius.
    
*   _Dispel Magic_ – Useful for removing enemy buffs or magical effects.
    
*   _Fly_ – Provides mobility.
    

#### **Level 4: - 2**

*   _Ice Storm_
    
*   _Wall of Fire_ – Battlefield control and continuous damage.
    
*   _Greater Invisibility_ – Cast it on yourself or an ally to remain undetected while casting spells.
    

### **Equipment:**

*   **Headband of Alluring Charisma +2** (or higher) – Boosts your Charisma, improving spell DCs and spells per day.
    
*   **Cloak of Resistance +2** – Helps with saving throws.
    
*   **Metamagic Rod (Lesser Empower)**.
