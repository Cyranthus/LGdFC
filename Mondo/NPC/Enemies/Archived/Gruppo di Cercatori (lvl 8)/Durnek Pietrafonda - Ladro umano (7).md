### **Class Features:**

*   **Sneak Attack (5d6):** Deals extra damage when flanking or targeting a foe denied its Dexterity bonus.
    
*   **Trapfinding:** +4 bonus to find/disarm traps, can find magical traps.
    
*   **Evasion:** No damage on a successful Reflex save.
    
*   **Trap Sense +3:** +3 bonus on Reflex saves and AC against traps.
    
*   **Uncanny Dodge:** Retains Dex bonus to AC when flat-footed.
    
*   **Improved Uncanny Dodge:** Cannot be flanked, except by rogues four levels higher.
    
*   **Rogue Talents:**
    
    *   **Combat Trick:** Gain a combat feat (Two-Weapon Fighting).
        
    *   **Finesse Rogue:** Can use Dexterity instead of Strength for melee attacks with light weapons.
        
    *   **Fast Stealth:** Move at full speed while using Stealth.
        
    *   **Crippling Strike:** Sneak attack deals 2 points of Strength damage.

### **Feats:**

*   **Weapon Finesse:** Use Dexterity instead of Strength on attack rolls with light weapons.
    
*   **Two-Weapon Fighting:** Make an extra attack when wielding two weapons, with a penalty.

### **Tactics:**

*   **Melee Combat:** This rogue uses Two-Weapon Fighting, relying on sneak attacks in flanking positions to maximize damage with short swords. The Improved Two-Weapon Fighting feat allows for multiple attacks, increasing their potential to land a sneak attack.
    
*   **Ranged Combat:** With Point-Blank Shot, Precise Shot, and Rapid Shot, this rogue can effectively switch to their composite bow for ranged combat, targeting enemies at a distance, especially while they’re distracted.
    
*   **Stealth and Mobility:** Fast Stealth lets the rogue move at full speed while remaining unseen. This, combined with high Acrobatics, allows them to navigate the battlefield efficiently.
    

This rogue build provides versatility between close-quarters fighting with two weapons and ranged attacks with a composite bow. It can engage enemies from various positions while benefiting from sneak attack bonuses.
