*   **Adaptability:** **Skill Focus (Use Magic Device)** as a bonus feat

*   **Multitalented:** Favored class bonuses in two classes (not utilized)

*   **Spells:** Casts sorcerer spells spontaneously; knows a limited number of spells

    *   **Woodland Stride (Ex):** Move through natural difficult terrain at normal speed
