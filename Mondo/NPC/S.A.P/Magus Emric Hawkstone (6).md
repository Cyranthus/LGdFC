*   **Arcane Pool:** 7 points per day to enhance his weapon or use magus arcana.
    
*   **Magus Arcana:**
    
    *   **Arcane Accuracy:** Spend 1 arcane point to add Intelligence bonus to attack rolls for one round.
        
    *   **Hasted Assault:** Expend 1 point to grant himself haste for one round.
        
*   **Spell Recall:** Use arcane pool to recall expended spells.
    
*   **Medium Armor Proficiency:** Can cast magus spells while wearing medium armor without incurring spell failure.

1.  **Weapon Finesse:** Use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls with light weapons.
    
2.  **Weapon Focus (Rapier):** +1 bonus on attack rolls with rapier.
    
3.  **Combat Casting:** +4 bonus on concentration checks when casting defensively.
    
4.  **Slashing Grace (applied to rapier):** Add Dexterity modifier to damage with rapier.
    
5.  **Intensified Spell:** Increase the maximum number of damage dice by 5 levels for spells like _shocking grasp_.
